Modelling Capabilities

Methods organised by problem type, not technical capability

Long-Term Capacity Planning

For multi-decade capacity expansion decisions under carbon constraints and technology cost uncertainty.

Methods

  • Linear and mixed-integer optimisation using TIMES, OSeMOSYS, or custom Pyomo/JuMP formulations
  • Multi-objective optimisation (cost, emissions, water use)
  • Technology learning curves and cost projections

Typical Applications

  • National energy system planning to 2050
  • Regional power sector expansion
  • Multi-sector integration (power, transport, industry)

Data Requirements

  • Historical energy demand and supply data (IEA, national statistics)
  • Technology cost and performance parameters
  • Carbon budgets and policy constraints

Hydrogen Infrastructure Under Uncertainty

For hydrogen production, storage, and transport infrastructure decisions when demand, prices, and renewable resource availability are uncertain.

Methods

  • Two-stage stochastic programming with CVaR risk measures
  • Scenario generation from historical data and projections
  • Robust optimisation for worst-case analysis

Typical Applications

  • Electrolyser sizing and technology selection
  • Pipeline and storage infrastructure planning
  • Export corridor optimisation (e.g., GCC to Europe)

Data Requirements

  • Hourly renewable energy profiles (solar, wind)
  • Electricity price time series
  • Demand scenarios and probability distributions

Technology Adoption and Market Dynamics

For understanding how technologies diffuse through markets and how policy interventions affect adoption rates.

Methods

  • Agent-based modelling calibrated against observed diffusion patterns
  • Bass diffusion models with heterogeneous agents
  • Network effects and spatial diffusion

Typical Applications

  • Heavy-duty vehicle fleet transitions
  • Residential solar PV adoption
  • Electric vehicle charging infrastructure roll-out

Data Requirements

  • Historical adoption data (NTRC, national vehicle registrations)
  • Agent characteristics and decision rules
  • Policy intervention timelines

Forecasting and Scenario Generation

For generating plausible future scenarios and integrating machine learning methods with optimisation frameworks.

Methods

  • Machine learning methods integrated with optimisation frameworks
  • Time series forecasting (ARIMA, LSTM) for demand and prices
  • Climate-adjusted renewable resource assessment using ERA5, NASA POWER

Typical Applications

  • Long-term demand forecasting
  • Climate-adjusted solar and wind resource assessment
  • Price scenario generation for stochastic models

Data Requirements

  • Historical time series (demand, prices, weather)
  • Climate reanalysis data (ERA5)
  • Satellite-derived solar irradiance (NASA POWER)

Technical Stack

Optimisation Frameworks

Pyomo (Python), JuMP (Julia), TIMES, OSeMOSYS, LEAP

Solvers

Gurobi, CPLEX, HiGHS, GLPK

Data Processing

Pandas, NumPy, CSV/Excel processing

Visualisation

Matplotlib, Plotly, Seaborn

Data Sources

ERA5, NASA POWER, IEA, IRENA, NEPRA, NTDC
