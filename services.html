Services

Optimisation-based analysis for energy system planning and investment decisions

1. Green Hydrogen Systems Analysis

Scope

Electrolyser sizing and dispatch optimisation, levelised cost of hydrogen (LCOH) calculations, storage and transport infrastructure planning. Focus areas include Pakistan freight transport applications and GCC export corridor analysis.

Deliverables

  • Optimal electrolyser capacity and technology selection
  • LCOH estimates under varying renewable energy and electricity price scenarios
  • Storage and pipeline infrastructure sizing recommendations
  • Comparison of business-as-usual and net-zero pathways with cost and emissions trajectories
  • Multi-objective trade-offs (cost, emissions, water use)

Methods

Mixed-integer linear programming for capacity expansion, hourly dispatch optimisation, two-stage stochastic programming for demand and price uncertainty.

2. Transport Sector Decarbonisation

Scope

Heavy-duty vehicle fleet transition modelling, charging infrastructure planning, total cost of ownership analysis for alternative fuel vehicles.

Deliverables

  • Optimal fleet transition pathways with timing and technology mix
  • Charging infrastructure location and capacity requirements
  • Total cost of ownership comparisons (diesel, electric, hydrogen fuel cell)
  • Agent-based diffusion models calibrated to observed adoption patterns
  • Policy intervention impact assessment

Methods

Agent-based modelling for technology adoption, MILP for infrastructure planning, scenario analysis for policy sensitivity.

3. Net-Zero Pathway Analysis

Scope

Long-term capacity expansion planning under carbon constraints, multi-sector integration (power, transport, industry), climate-adjusted renewable energy resource assessment.

Deliverables

  • Technology mix and capacity expansion schedules to 2050
  • Annual cost and emissions trajectories
  • Sensitivity analysis on carbon budgets and technology costs
  • Climate-adjusted solar and wind resource assessments
  • Water-energy nexus analysis where relevant

Methods

TIMES, OSeMOSYS, or custom Pyomo/JuMP formulations for long-term planning, ERA5 and NASA POWER data processing for climate-adjusted resources.

4. Risk and Uncertainty Analysis

Scope

Stochastic optimisation under demand, price, and resource uncertainty. Conditional Value-at-Risk (CVaR) formulations for risk-averse decision-making.

Deliverables

  • Two-stage stochastic programming models with scenario generation
  • Risk-averse solutions using CVaR measures
  • Value of stochastic solution (VSS) and expected value of perfect information (EVPI)
  • Sensitivity analysis on risk parameters
  • Robustness testing across scenario sets

Methods

Two-stage stochastic MILP, scenario reduction techniques, Monte Carlo simulation for uncertainty propagation.

5. Custom Optimisation Models

Scope

Development of custom optimisation models for specific decision problems not covered by standard frameworks.

Deliverables

  • Mathematical formulation in LaTeX notation
  • Pyomo or JuMP implementation with solver integration
  • Model documentation with assumption registers
  • Validation and sensitivity analysis results
  • Reproducible code repository

Methods

Problem-specific mathematical programming formulations, solver selection (Gurobi, CPLEX, HiGHS) based on problem structure and scale.
