About

Dr. Gordhan Das Valasai is Associate Professor of Mechanical Engineering at Quaid-e-Awam University of Engineering, Science & Technology (QUEST), Nawabshah, Pakistan. Research focuses on green hydrogen pathways, transport-sector decarbonisation, and net-zero transition analysis using TIMES, LEAP, OSeMOSYS, and Pyomo optimisation frameworks.

Geographic and Sectoral Experience

Energy system models developed for Pakistan's power sector, rural electrification in Tharparkar, and hydrogen infrastructure planning. International research collaboration includes work at Hacettepe University, Ankara (Türkiye) on TIMES/MARKAL electricity demand modelling. Focus areas include hydrogen infrastructure, transport fleet transitions, and national energy planning under carbon constraints.

International Collaborations and Funding

Principal Investigator for British Council Grant (£5,930) on off-grid solar PV electrification in rural Pakistan. Published 30+ peer-reviewed papers (1,920 citations; h-index 17) on energy system optimisation and policy analysis.
