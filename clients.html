Who I Work With

I Work With

  • Government agencies and policy makers requiring evidence-based analysis for energy transition planning
  • Investment firms and project developers evaluating hydrogen infrastructure and renewable energy projects
  • Research institutions and universities conducting energy system analysis
  • International organisations developing energy transition strategies
  • Technology companies assessing market opportunities and infrastructure requirements

What I Offer

Work is structured around optimisation-based analysis with transparent assumptions, reproducible methods, and clear documentation of limitations.

  • Mathematical optimisation models (MILP, stochastic programming) for capacity expansion and dispatch decisions
  • Agent-based models for technology adoption and market dynamics
  • Scenario analysis and sensitivity testing
  • Full model documentation with assumption registers
  • Reproducible code repositories
  • Validation checks and independent review support

What I Do Not Offer

I do not offer generic feasibility studies or Excel-only analyses.

Projects requiring only spreadsheet-based calculations, simple cost-benefit analysis without optimisation, or generic market research are outside the scope of this consultancy.

Project Scope

Typical projects involve:

  • Complex decision problems requiring mathematical optimisation
  • Multi-objective trade-offs (cost, emissions, resource use)
  • Uncertainty quantification and risk analysis
  • Long-term planning horizons (10-30 years)
  • Integration of multiple energy sectors or technologies
